# Policy for Control: SC.L2-3.13.14 (FII: FII-SCF-NET-0013)

As electronic messaging systems are critical for internal and external communications, safeguarding these channels against unauthorized access and data breaches is paramount to maintaining operational security and compliance with CMMC guidelines.

## 2. Policy Statement
The organization is dedicated to ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all electronic messaging communications. This includes implementing technical and administrative safeguards to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction of sensitive information transmitted via electronic messaging systems.

## 3. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party vendors who utilize electronic messaging systems, including but not limited to cloud-hosted systems, SaaS applications, and any third-party vendor systems that handle organizational communications. It encompasses all forms of electronic messaging, including email, instant messaging, and collaboration platforms.

## 4. Responsibilities
- **IT Security Team**: Conduct **Monthly Audits** of electronic messaging systems to ensure compliance with this policy.
- **Compliance Officer**: Review and update the policy on an **Annual Review** basis to reflect changes in regulatory requirements and organizational needs.
- **Network Administrators**: Implement encryption protocols for electronic messaging systems **Quarterly** to enhance security.
- **All Employees**: Complete training on electronic messaging security protocols **Annually** to ensure awareness and compliance.
