# Compliance Policy for Control CA.L2-3.12.1 (FII: FII-SCF-CPL-0003)

## Introduction
This policy outlines the framework for ensuring that managers regularly review processes and documented procedures within their areas of responsibility to maintain adherence to cybersecurity and data protection policies, standards, and other applicable requirements. The objective is to foster a culture of compliance and continuous improvement in safeguarding electronic protected health information (ePHI).

## Policy Statement
The organization is committed to implementing a structured review process that guarantees regular evaluation of policies and procedures. This commitment aims to ensure compliance with cybersecurity and data protection standards, thereby protecting sensitive information and maintaining trust with stakeholders.

## Scope
This policy applies to all organizational entities, including but not limited to:
- Cloud-hosted systems
- SaaS applications
- Third-party vendor systems
- All communication channels utilized to create, receive, maintain, or transmit ePHI

## Responsibilities
- **Managers**: Review processes and procedures **Quarterly** to ensure compliance with applicable standards.
- **Compliance Team**: Conduct audits and provide reports **Biannually** to assess adherence to the policy.
- **IT Department**: Support evidence collection efforts **Monthly** by maintaining logs and automated systems.

## Evidence Collection Methods

### 1. REQUIREMENT
Managers must regularly review processes and documented procedures to ensure compliance with established cybersecurity and data protection policies.

### 2. MACHINE ATTESTATION
- Utilize automated compliance management tools to schedule and log reviews.
- Implement monitoring systems that track changes in policies and procedures, generating alerts for needed reviews.

### 3. HUMAN ATTESTATION
- Managers must complete a standardized review checklist and submit it via the Surveilr platform.
- Documented evidence of reviews, including meeting minutes and action items, must be uploaded into Surveilr for verification.

## Verification Criteria
- Reviews conducted on schedule as evidenced by logs from compliance tools.
- Completion of manager checklists submitted via Surveilr.
- Audit findings from the Compliance Team indicating adherence to the review process.

## Exceptions
Any exceptions to this policy must be formally documented and approved by the Compliance Team. Exceptions will be reviewed and assessed on a case-by-case basis.

## Lifecycle Requirements
- **Data Retention**: Maintain evidence and logs for a minimum of **three years**.
- **Annual Review**: This policy will be reviewed and updated at least **Annually** to ensure ongoing effectiveness and relevance.

## Formal Documentation and Audit
All workforce members must acknowledge and attest to their understanding and compliance with this policy. Comprehensive audit logs must be maintained for all critical actions related to the review process, and formal documentation must be created for any exceptions identified.
